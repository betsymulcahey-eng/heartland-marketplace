How VIP Outlet Deals Works

Everything in one place — where deals show up, how to buy, and what to expect.

Where deals show up

This website — the full current selection, organized by category, with search and sorting. Flooring has its own page grouped by brand.

The VIP Facebook Group — where our Thursday auction happens live, and where you'll see new arrivals first.

Facebook Marketplace — a lot of the same inventory is also listed here for anyone who prefers browsing that way.

The Updates blog — product spotlights and what's new at Heartland Outlet.

How to buy something

1. Browse

Find something you want on the site, in the Facebook group, or on Marketplace.

2. Message

Click "I'm Interested" or send a message. We'll confirm stock and condition, then send a simple invoice.

3. Pick up

Pay the invoice and pick up during store hours at Heartland Outlet, 1698 Vierling Drive E, Shakopee, MN.

Store Hours: Tue & Thu 12–7pm • Fri & Sat 9am–12pm

The Thursday auction

Every Thursday, new items go live in the VIP Facebook Group as part of our auction. It's the fastest way to see what's new and get first pick before things move to the website or Marketplace.

A couple of important notes

We do our best to remove sold items right away, but availability can occasionally lag behind real-time inventory. Click "I'm Interested" on any item to confirm current stock and condition before you make plans to purchase.

"Coming Soon" items reflect what we anticipate having available. Because every shipment is inspected upon arrival, exact availability, condition, and contents can vary from what's listed, and nothing is guaranteed until it's physically in stock.

Prices are firm since everything is already deeply discounted — no negotiating over messaging.
