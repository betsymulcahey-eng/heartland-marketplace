Tired of flooring that looks good but can't handle spills, pets, kids, or moisture? This is the good stuff.

Monolithic means the entire plank is made from one single, solid piece of material all the way through — not layered like traditional flooring — so it's 100% waterproof from top to bottom.

Super simple, super tough.

It gives you that authentic hardwood look and feel — a high-definition wood grain photo layer plus a realistic embossed-in-register (EIR) texture you can actually run your hand over — but the entire plank is one solid, engineered piece, not a thin wood layer on top of something else.
